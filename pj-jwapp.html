Juwai app Redesign

The objective of the Juwai app redesign project is to enhance the app's performance, optimize user experience, boost traffic, and drive conversion by meeting the evolving needs of our property buyers.

Employer

Juwai

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

2016.7 - 2022.1

Delieverables

Mobile App

Homepage

We have improved the navigation menu to cater to different user search paths. We have also enriched the content to provide more information about Juwai's services and products, aiding buyers in purchasing overseas property.

The homepage offers multiple approaches to help buyers explore property listings. In the upper section, buyers can find rotating banners, springboards for property channels, and country property entrances. These options cater to buyers who have different preferences and search criteria.

In the middle section, buyers can access a wealth of property buying news and resources, including market headlines, reports, charts, and encyclopedias. By consolidating this valuable content, buyers can stay updated and make knowledgeable decisions.

Finally, in the last section, buyers are presented with real estate listings. These listings are meant to entice buyers and encourage them to begin their property search.

Property Search

Our revamped search page offers enhanced capabilities for effortless property exploration. Buyers can easily filter their search by location, price range, and property type, refining options to find the perfect match.

Property Channels

To ensure a positive search experience and improve visibility, we have created distinct channels for new projects, luxury houses, and commercial properties, each featuring tailored content. This allows buyers to easily navigate and find the specific type of property they are interested in.

Property Details

Buyers can explore properties through various mediums like virtual reality (VR), videos, and photos. All textual information is meticulously organized and presented in a user-friendly format, ensuring easy readability. This immersive experience empowers buyers to grasp a comprehensive understanding of the property, enabling them to make inquiries.

Agent Profile

Our agent profile page showcases essential details about the agent/ company, providing property buyers with insights into their background and expertise. Additionally, it conveniently displays the agent's current property listings, allowing buyers to explore a wide range of options in one place. By connecting property details with the agent profile, buyers can easily reach out to knowledgeable professionals, gain a deeper understanding of the listings, and make well-educated decisions with confidence.

Enquiry Box Module

To keep our UI organized and offer a consistent user experience across our app, we built a single enquiry module.

This not only simplifies the user experience but also streamlines the development process. It enables easier maintenance and updates, ensuring a cohesive and efficient user journey across the app.

News and Report

Well-Organized News

Our app allows buyers to focus on news items relevant to their real estate interests, ensuring they never miss important information.

Reports and Guides

Buyers can access valuable insights and knowledge by downloading our publications and market research materials through our Reports and Guides Channel, empowering them to make thoughtful decisions.

Juwai.asia

Asia Real Estate Platform

Juwai.asia is the extended platform of Juwai.com, where we expanded our business from China to Asia in 2019. Within Juwai.asia, our team faced the challenge of reaching a broader target audience with tighter deadlines. Here is the case study of this project from the perspective of a solo product designer.

Project Requirements & Constraints

In line with the company's objective to launch the platform within 3 months, it is essential to identify the necessary features and enhancements required for a successful rollout.

As an extension of Juwai.com, Juwai.asia shares some similarities in features while also introducing new elements. To align with our business objectives, Juwai.asia must encompass the fundamental features present on the Chinese platform upon its initial release:

  • property searching
  • global real estate market news
  • consulting services

Distinctive features for Juwai.asia include:

  • Customized homepage content based on buyer location
  • Multi-language and multi-currency support within the UI
  • Ability for buyers to search for local property (a feature not available on the Chinese platform)
  • Development of a responsive website for optimal user experience

Final Design

Global Gateway

Since Juwai.asia's content, language, and currency are customized based on the buyer's location, we need to determine the buyer's location when they first land on our website. The site will then remember the selected location using cookies.

Customized Homepage

The homepage's user interface (UI) is customized based on the buyer's location. In most locations, the homepages will feature a mix of global and local properties. However, in certain regions, only local properties are showcased.

This tailored approach aligns with the company's business strategy, which aims to cater to the specific needs and preferences of buyers in different locations.

Global Search Page

Developing the Global Search Page involved understanding diverse buyer search behaviors worldwide. To address this, we researched different property search sites and consulted our sales team. Here are the key features:

The Rental Option Filter

Based on our research, we discovered that in addition to monthly rentals, there are also weekly and yearly rental options. Therefore, we expanded the available choices to meet diverse preferences and needs.

Currency Formatting

To enhance readability and accommodate currency adjustments, we implemented a smart rounding system. This maintains clarity by avoiding long numbers, making it easier for buyers to compare prices and make decisions.

Property Details Page

In comparison to Juwai.com, the Property Details Page on Juwai.asia has undergone minimal changes. However, to better accommodate the diverse searching behaviors of buyers worldwide, we have introduced some subtle enhancements:

Unit Conversion

Buyers can now seamlessly switch between various measurement units (such as square feet and square meters) to cater to regional preferences.

Local Real Estate Market Insights

At the bottom of each property details page, buyers can easily access valuable information on local real estate market trends, pricing dynamics, and investment opportunities. This provides buyers with insightful data to make informed decisions.

The mentioned pages and features represent significant differences when compared to the Chinese site Juwai.com. The remaining pages, such as Property Channels, Market News, Membership Pages, etc., have also been successfully implemented as per the roadmap, mirroring the Chinese site.

What's Next?

Indeed, people from various countries/regions exhibit distinct property searching and buying behaviors that cannot be effectively addressed using a uniform approach. An ideal strategy would involve selecting specific locations for focused analysis of their unique buying processes. Subsequently, adjustments can be made to tailor the overall experience to the individual needs of each location.

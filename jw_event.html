Juwai hosts events for property buyers to learn about the overseas property market, but due to a lack of promotion, the attendance rate was poor. In order to remedy the situation, we created a brand-new event channel on Juwai.com, which will serve as a hub for promoting and organizing events. Additionally, we developed a content management system (CMS) to facilitate team management of the channel.

Project Scope

ˇ
  • Promoting Events
  • Current Problems
  • Publishing the event promotion landing pages together with the daily news page:
    • Overshadowing: The event promotion landing pages get overshadowed by the daily news, leading to lower visibility and reduced attention from the audience
    • Lower Conversion: The presence of event promotion alongside daily news might lead to a lower conversion rate as visitors may be less inclined to engage with promotional content while seeking daily news updates
  • Product Goal
  • Introduce a new, dedicated channel for event promotion to showcase upcoming events, mitigating the overshadowing effect caused by daily news and improving the conversion rate by providing a distinct platform for user engagement.
  • Managing Event Landing Pages
  • Current Problems
  • The marketing team's inability to manage event pages independently:
    • Dependency: Creates a dependency on the design and development teams, potentially leading to delays in implementing real-time adjustments or updates
    • Communication Challenges: Coordinating between teams may cause inefficiencies and misunderstandings
  • Product Goal
  • Develop a CMS tailored for the marketing team, enabling independent management of event landing pages. This solution aims to streamline real-time adjustments and updates, as well as enhance overall productivity and effectiveness.

Final Design

Event Landing Page Template

Based on our study of current landing pages, we have developed a responsive landing page template incorporating the sections we identified.

The well-organized page enables visitors to navigate and access comprehensive event details, ultimately leading to increased participation rates.

Effortless Event Registration Access

The registration button is conveniently positioned on the sticky top bar on the landing page, ensuring easy access for buyers to register for the event.

Final Design

Event Channel CMS

In order to streamline the event management process, we have developed a CMS for marketing team to independently manage both the event channel and the event landing pages.

The event landing page template is divided into sections, each designed as modules with pre-defined layouts. The team can easily modify the text and upload images using the input fields within the CMS.

Once the landing page is published through the CMS, it will automatically generate and appear on the Event Channel, ensuring a seamless and efficient process for displaying event information.

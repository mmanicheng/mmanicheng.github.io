Define Problem

We interviewed existing users and property agents to observe how they use the app to complete the given user tasks. We discovered the following problems of the app

• Slow speed
• Insufficient features to help decision making
• Insufficient market info compared to PC site
• Outdated user interfaces

Homepage

The homepage was divided into several sections to present information that needed from different user groups.

The upper sections are slideshow, springboard and country shortcuts, which tailored for buyers who are searching for oversea properties. These entry points guide buyers to find properties in different property types, countries or from latest conference and event.

The middle section is information based e.g. news, knowledge database, reports, charts etc. It targets buyers who are doing their research or reading before making purchase.

The bottom part of every section has a button to bring buyers to browse further in order to continue their journey on our app.

Property Search

When buyers search for properties, they will land on this page to browse and filter the property listings around the world on this page.

The filters and list view were designed in minimal style, so buyers can focus on the property details and images, those necessary to their decision making process.

Besides, buyers can save the selected filters and favourite properties. So they can re-apply the filter or re-visit the saved properties later on. 

Property Details Page

After choosing a property listing from search page, buyers will land on the individual property details page that shows the house with multimedia like VR, photos, videos and map.

At the bottom of the page, we added some latest reports and brochures about the country or region where this property locates,.

WeChat Mini Program for Buyer Referral


After Juwai App is revamped and relaunched, we developed a WeChat Program JCAN (Juwai Chinese Agent Network) to connect property agents for buyer referral.

WeChat Mini Program (小程序) is the app that user do not need to install, but can use directly within WeChat. It is suitable for light app that seldom use or the app that needs to operate with WeChat.

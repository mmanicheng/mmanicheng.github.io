Homepage Revamp

Overview

Juwai.com, originally designed as a property searching platform, had a previous homepage that resembled a news site due to its abundance of real estate news. Unfortunately, this layout led to a subpar user experience when it came to property searching.

To address this issue, we made the decision to revamp the homepage in order to realign it with Juwai's core objective as a property searching platform and effectively cater to the demands of our buyers.

Homepage Revamp

Search Suggestions

The search field is positioned prominently in the central area at the top of the page, catching buyers' eye and guiding them towards the search section. By clicking on the search field, a dropdown menu appears, displaying the most popular keywords and recent searches, which provides quick access to popular topics and recent searches without typing.

Homepage Revamp

Country Selection Panel

The panel is displayed above the banner as the starting point for buyers to embark on their search journey by selecting their preferred nations or regions of interest.

Homepage Revamp

Overall Redesign

The revamped homepage works like a dashboard for property buyers, allowing them to choose their own adventure by either hunting for properties or gaining market insights. The layout emphasizes the equal importance of both property search and market information sections, providing users with a well-balanced experience.

Event Channel & CMS

Overview

Juwai hosts events for property buyers to learn about the overseas property market, but due to a lack of promotion, the attendance rate was poor.

In order to remedy the situation, we created a brand-new event channel on Juwai.com, which will serve as a hub for promoting and organizing upcoming events. Additionally, we developed a content management system (CMS) to facilitate team management of the channel.

Event Channel & CMS

Event Channel Homepage

On the top of the event channel homepage, a rotating banner was implemented to showcase featured and upcoming events, aiming to increase their exposure and participation rate. This was followed by a grid view of event thumbnails, designed to resemble movie posters, in order to enhance recognition and engage attendees. Visitors are able to filter the events based on their own needs using the filter panel located on the left side.

Event Channel & CMS

Event Landing Page

Once visitors click on the event thumbnail, they will land on the event landing page, which consists of 9 sections:

  1. Banner
  2. Name/Venue/Date
  3. Event Overview
  4. Detailed Introduction
  5. Photo Album
  6. Speakers/Guests
  7. Event Timetable
  8. Organizers
  9. Press Media

The page is well-organized, allowing visitors to navigate effortlessly and learn about the event details, ultimately increasing the participation rate.

Furthermore, the registration button is conveniently placed on the sticky top bar, ensuring easy accessibility for buyers to register for the event.

Event Channel & CMS

CMS

In order to streamline the event management process, we have developed a CMS (Content Management System) for the marketing team to independently manage both the event channel and the event landing page.

The event landing page is divided into sections, each designed as modules with pre-defined layouts. The team can easily modify the text and upload images using the pre-defined input fields within the CMS.

Once the landing page is published through the CMS, it will automatically generate and appear on the Event Channel, ensuring a seamless and efficient process for displaying event information.

Encyclopaedia &
landing pages

Overview

More than 70% of buyers discover Juwai.com through search queries related to their purchasing intentions. However, our content has been disorganized and challenging to locate, resulting in buyers leaving the site.

To address this issue, we have introduced the Encyclopaedia Channel and buying motive landing pages on Juwai.com. These additions aim to bridge the gap by providing buyers with a dedicated platform that caters to their specific purchasing needs.

Encyclopaedia & landing pages

Encyclopaedia

Originally, all property market updates, including policies, restrictions, and buying procedures, were only presented in daily news. This made it challenging for buyers to conduct research in such a vast sea of content.

However, on our new Encyclopaedia channel, we have organized the information in the following hierarchy: purchasing intentions > countries > purchasing procedures > articles

This allows buyers to easily access the relevant information they need to make informed decisions about purchasing property in a specific country. By providing organized and comprehensive details, we aim to facilitate the research and decision-making process for buyers, ensuring they have the necessary information at their fingertips.

Encyclopaedia & landing pages

Landing pages

Based on our study, we created 4 property buying intention landing pages: investment, education, immigration, lifestyle. Each landing page features a consistent layout and structure, presenting tailored content related to its specific theme.

These pages serve as comprehensive dashboards, providing buyers with access to hot topics, events, property recommendations, policies, and encyclopaedia entries relevant to their buying intentions.

Property buyers can easily choose their starting point and begin their journey based on their interests and goals, facilitating efficient information retrieval.

Video Channel

Overview

We have developed a dedicated video page on Juwai.com, consolidating a wide range of video content. This project aims to provide buyers with a seamless and comprehensive video experience, offering valuable insights and engaging visuals. From a business perspective, this strategic initiative enhances user engagement, boosts brand visibility, and strengthens our position as a leading platform in the real estate market.

Video Channel

Channel Homepage

It is a centralized hub that brings together various types of video content for property buyers. It includes highlights from real estate events, interviews with industry experts, informative sharing sessions, and engaging property tours. Whether property buyers are looking for market trends, expert advice, or virtual property tours, our video channel homepage offers a diverse collection of videos to cater to their needs.

Video Channel

Video page

The content will vary depending on the type of video. For instance, an event highlight video will include a link to the event landing page, allowing buyers to explore information about the speakers and other event details. On the other hand, a property tour video will provide a link back to the property details page and the agent profile, enabling buyers to access more comprehensive information about the property being showcased.

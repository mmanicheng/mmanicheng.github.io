Properity Marketing Kits

For Online Property Development Event

In this design project, I crafted marketing kits to drive promotion for an online event dedicated to introducing a new property development to the audience in Hong Kong. These kits encompassed social media ads, a responsive landing page, and an Electronic Direct Mail (EDM) page. The design approach integrated a modern and sophisticated visual style to convey the upscale essence of the property development, aiming to entice potential buyers and investors with a compelling and luxurious aesthetic.

about

This project features a tablet-friendly B2B dashboard, created for property listings advertisers on Juwai.com. The main goal was to convert a static report into a dynamic, easy-to-use interface, giving advertisers clear insights into their property listing performance. It’s designed specifically for businesses aiming to boost their online property marketing.

Advertisers can instantly see important data like market share, listing views, leads, and user engagement. The dashboard provides a simple overview of key actions—such as clicks, phone reveals, and website redirects—helping businesses evaluate how well their property listings are performing. It also includes data on ad campaigns, offering insights into conversion rates, click-through rates, and the effectiveness of different channels like SEM, EDM, and social media.

This B2B platform empowers property advertisers by providing them with real-time data and easy-to-read visual reports. With these insights, they can make better marketing decisions, improve their listings, and get more value from their advertising efforts on Juwai.com.

Buying Motive Web Design
UI/UX, Graphic Design   •    Sketch, PS, AI

This is an enhancement project to optimise our user experience on Juwai.com. Based on the buying motives of property buyers, our team and I designed 4 landing pages to present information that tailored for buyers with different needs in order to guide them to where they are looking for. These information are consolidated on 4 Motive Landing Pages and related knowledge database.

Buying Motives Landing Pages

Buyers search for property with different needs and motives. We designed 4 Landing Pages accordingly, they were designed with the same layout but presented with different information  based on the buying motives:

  • Oversea Investment
  • Study Aboard
  • Immigration
  • Buy for retirement/lifestyle

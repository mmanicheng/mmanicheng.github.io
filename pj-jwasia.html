Global Gateway

The global gateway serves as a powerful tool to enhance the property buying experience for our Asia user base. It enables property buyers from different countries and language backgrounds to effortlessly access localized information, explore listings in their preferred language, and find properties that meet their specific requirements.

  1. Verify Location
    Our site detects buyers' location based on their IP address, ensuring they land on the right homepage at Juwai.asia.
  2. Choose Location
    Buyers can easily re-select their desired location or confirm the detected one, allowing them to explore different realms for property search.
  3. Country Homepage
    A single click transports buyers to the captivating Country Homepage of their chosen realm. Their selection is saved in their browser's cookie for future visits, eliminating the need to repeat this process.

Country Homepage

Buyers could see campaigns, property recommendations, news, and brands tailored specifically for their chosen area on the homepage. These were displayed in the local currency and languages.

This tailored-made homepage enhances user experience, improves content relevance, and facilitates efficient decision-making for buyers in their property search journey.

This profile page serves as a valuable resource for buyers, allowing them to explore the company background, property listings, agent profiles, and team members in their preferred language.

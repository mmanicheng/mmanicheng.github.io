Juwai Mobile App

A Leading Chinese Real Estate App Revamp

Juwai, China's leading Overseas Property Searching Online Platform, serves as a bridge connecting Chinese property buyers and investors with overseas properties. It enables Chinese buyers to access overseas properties in Chinese, while allowing global agents to showcase properties to Chinese buyers. This eliminates language barriers and navigates around the Great Firewall of China.

However, the mobile app had not been updated for a long time and was left abandoned. Juwai made the decision to revive the app by giving it a fresh update. The main goal of this Juwai app redesign project is to improve the app's performance, enhance user experience, increase traffic, and enhance conversion rates by aligning with the changing requirements of our property buyers. In this project, my role was the UI and UX designer, overseeing the entire design process.

Final Design

Onboarding Pages

Three onboarding pages were added to the app launch to provide updates on our app. This initiative informed users that our app had been enhanced with not only the interfaces but also more features to support their property searching and buying journey.

Homepage

To enhance the overseas property searching experience for Chinese buyers, our enriched homepage offers:

Multiple Browsing Approaches

Featuring banners, navigation tools, and a country search gateway in the top section, catering to diverse preferences.

Diverse Buying information

Providing property buying news and resources in the middle section to keep buyers informed and empowered to make well-informed decisions.

Recommended Properties

Showcasing real estate listings in the last section to captivate buyers and motivate them to kickstart their property search journey.

The redesigned homepage not only caters to the diverse needs and preferences of our users but also showcases how Juwai supports their buying journey from various perspectives.

Property Search

The enhanced search page elevates the property exploration experience by introducing categorized filter options, aiding buyers in refining their searches effortlessly to discover their ideal match. Additionally, a prominent search box at the top enables users to swiftly input their queries.

Property Details

Since our targeted users are Chinese buyers looking for overseas property, the main goal of the details page is to help buyers learn about the property as much as possible without visiting in person.

Upon visiting the details page, buyers can initiate a virtual property tour using different mediums like virtual reality (VR), videos, and photos.

Furthermore, they can explore in-depth details including pricing, address, interior design, and surrounding amenities.

Lastly, buyers will receive suggestions for nearby property listings to keep them engaged in their search. This immersive experience empowers buyers to gain a comprehensive understanding of the property and make inquiries using the bottom sticky tab bar.

Agent Profile Page

Buyers can visit the property's agent or agency profile from the details page to understand their service areas, contact details, and connections, providing an overview of their capabilities and reach.

Additionally, accessing their represented property listings allows for a more targeted and informed property search, offering insights into the types of properties they specialize in and enhancing the overall property exploration experience.

Enquiry Module

To keep our UI organized and offer a consistent user experience across our app, we built a single enquiry module. This not only simplifies the user experience but also streamlines the development process. It enables easier maintenance and updates, ensuring a cohesive and efficient user journey across the app.

Editorials

On the editorial page, news and reports are categorized to align with buyers' interests, saving them time and effort in finding relevant information.

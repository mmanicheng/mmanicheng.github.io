Project Background

Initially, our client purchased a workforce management web system to manage working schedule for their part-time employees.
However, the HR team discovered new issues after they used the new system for awhile:

  • Ongoing Edits on Timesheet
    Employees always swap shift with their coworkers after HR published the 1st timesheet. HR had to update and re-print the timesheet after every edit again and again. This ongoing edits burdened HR team's workload and they were not happy with the new system which was too expensive to abandon.
  • Do not Support Complex Logics for Leave Types
    Our client has complicated rules and conditions to different leave types for different teams but the system couldn't handle such situation. HR team had to check c and approve every leave submission manually.
  • Steep Learning Curve
    The Adobe Flash developed system is slow and the interfaces are complicated for administration who have been using Excel to manage workforce for years. They needed to spend extra working hours to learn and adapt.

Define & Ideate

Before jumping into solutions, we arranged weekly workshops with our focus group of part-time employees.
We wanted to investigate their interaction with the new system and how it might involve in their daily tasks. 
We used sticky notes and whiteboard to illustrate diagrams to consolidate and analyse the collected data, in order to generate ideas and solutions.

Wireframe

After that, I led our team to draft out wireframe of our solutions, illustrated task flows and came up with necessary features that valuable to our users.

Working Schedule

Once employees logged in to their account, the app will show their own working schedule in the form of calendar on the front page. They could:

  • Check up-to-dated working schedules
    All timesheet edits from client's external web system will be sync to the calendar in real time
  • Check clock in/out time 
    Easily keep track and submit notes for exceptional cases
  • Swap shift with coworkers
    Submit the shift swap requests and sync the changes to calendar and web system

Leave Application and Records

Employees could submit leave request by following the preset rules and keep track on the approval status of submitted requests.

All approved leaves will be reflected on the web system and working calendar.

Leave Configuration Plugin

This powerful leave configuration module was installed on the web system that the company purchased. This helps HR to set up complicated rules for different leave types. 

All rules will be apply and synchronise to the employee app and the web system, employee could apply for leave accordingly. 

Once the rules were set, administration don't need to handle leave requests manually that highly lighten their workload.

ezWFM app + Kronos Plugin

Workforce Management Platform

ezWFM, short for 'Easy Workforce Management,' encompasses an staff app and an administrative webpage plugin. This project served as a technical solution that we developed for our client, a Hong Kong-based airline cargo company, to alleviate their significant workload challenges in managing their workforce.

Research and Analysis

Focus Group Workshop

ˇ

At the start of the project, we organized weekly focus group workshops with the app users, sspecially the client's staff who follow a set roster. Our main objective was to understand the challenges they face when it comes to tasks such as checking their roster, applying for leave, and other related activities.

During these workshops, we utilized sticky notes and a whiteboard to gather and consolidate data from the focus group. We have learned that every time a new roster is prepared, HR prints a few hard copies and displays them in the office for staff to review. If staff wish to swap shifts or request leave, they must obtain approval from their supervisor. Upon receiving approval, they make manual changes to the printed roster, resulting in a messy document. HR is then required to collect these copies to calculate payroll each month. This is painful to everyone involved.

Similar problem also happened for clock in/out process. If a staff's clock in/out time doesn't align with the scheduled roster, they are required to fill out a paper form explaining the discrepancy and submit it to their supervisor and HR. Failure to do so may impact their payroll.

Final Design

Staff App

Real time Roster

After logging in, staff will land on the calendar page as the initial app screen. The calendar will display their most recent roster. Any timesheet adjustments made in the HR system will be synchronized with each staff’s calendar to ensure accuracy.

Shift Swapping

Staff can easily request a shift swap by sending a request in the calendar with their colleague's name. Once the request is accepted by the colleague and approved by the manager, the updated response will be reflected in both the app and the HR system.

Clock Time

The app syncs staff clock times from the client's machine to their calendars. In case of a mismatch, the app prompts the staff for an explanation. Staff can conveniently submit notes for managers and HR to review. This streamlined process ensures accurate payroll calculations, benefiting all parties involved.

Leave Application and Records

Staffs can submit leave requests following the preset rules, streamlining the leave request process and ensuring efficient documentation of absences.

They can keep track of the approval status of their submitted requests, and once approved, the leave will be accurately reflected on the HR system and staff’s working calendar for seamless coordination.

Kronos Plugin

Leave Configuration

We designed a powerful leave configuration plugin and installed to existing HR system, Kronos, enabling HR to establish different rules for various leave types. These rules are then applied and synchronized with the staff app, allowing staffs to submit leave requests accordingly.

Next Steps of the Project

We have successfully implemented the essential features and workflow to address our client's high-priority challenges. As the app serves as an enhancement to Kronos (the HR web system), certain processes such as manager and HR approval workflows continue to be handled within the existing system. However, we envision the potential to elevate this project into a standalone product by developing a dedicated app for managers, incorporating the following key features:

  • Calendar – Team Roster
  • Leave Approval
  • Shift Swap Approval

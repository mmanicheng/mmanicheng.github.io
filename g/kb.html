about

KooBits is a Singapore-based learning website for children aged 6-12 to learn Maths and Science. My role involved designing Chinese banner ads for the Hong Kong market, where the company planned to expand. As a local Hong Kong designer, I have a deep understanding of what appeals to Hong Kong audiences and what kind of banner designs capture their attention. This local expertise was why I was brought on to help with the company's business expansion.

The deliverables included static banner ads and video ads published on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.
